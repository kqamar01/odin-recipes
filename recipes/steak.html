Beef Stroganoff

Desctiption

Ingredients

  • 2 ribeye steaks (about 1 inch thick)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon fresh thyme, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Lemon wedges (for serving)

Step by Step

  1. Remove the steaks from the refrigerator and let them come to room temperature for about 30 minutes. This ensures even cooking.
  2. Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C).
  3. Pat the steaks dry with paper towels to remove any excess moisture. This helps achieve a good sear.
  4. Rub the steaks with olive oil on both sides. Season generously with salt and pepper.
  5. Heat a large oven-safe skillet over high heat until it's very hot. Add the steaks to the skillet and sear for 2-3 minutes on each side, until a golden-brown crust forms.
  6. Add the minced garlic, chopped rosemary, and thyme to the skillet. Place a tablespoon of butter on each steak.
  7. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and cook for an additional 5-7 minutes for medium-rare, or until the steaks reach your desired level of doneness.
  8. Remove the skillet from the oven and transfer the steaks to a cutting board. Let them rest for 5-10 minutes to allow the juices to redistribute.
  9. Serve the steaks with lemon wedges and your favorite sides.
